The Digital Youth Network

Our efforts to create frameworks to understand 21st century learning online builds on the Digital Youth Network's work helping economically disadvantaged middle-school students in Chicago to develop technical, creative, and analytical skills across a learning ecology that spans school, community, home, and online.

Read more about DYN in the book: 

Baron, B., Gomez, K., Pinkard, N., & Martin, C. K. (2014). The Digital Youth Network: Cultivating digital media citizenship in urban communities. MIT Press.
