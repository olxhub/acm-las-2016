DEER: Facilitating Distributed Assessment by NON-EXPERTS!

The ASSESSOR is:
  • Recruited by the MOOC participant
  • Located geographically close to the MOOC participant
  • A respected member of the participant's community
  • Not necessarily an expert in MOOC content
The MOOC provides:
  • Project instructions for the PARTICIPANT which require understanding of MOOC content
  • Assessment instructions for the ASSESSOR which can be performed without content knowledge
  • A certificate that is only "valid" when endorsed by the assessor.

The DEER review was developed for our EDUCATIONAL ROBOTS FOR ABSOLUTE BEGINNERS MOOCs which are designed to introduce K-12 teachers to LEGO NXT and LEGO EV3 Robot programming languages

  • Both the NXT Programming Language and the EV3 Programming Language are visual block-based languages
  • When MOOC participants affirm that they have completed the MOOC, they are permitted to download a certificate of completion
  • BUT, the certificate says "Not valid without principal's signature"!
  • The principal is asked to assess whether participants have completed the projects based on robot behavior rather than on an understanding of the programming itself.
