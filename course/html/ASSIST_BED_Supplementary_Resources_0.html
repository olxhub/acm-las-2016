PLACEments and Curriculum Ordering

Interested in helping to determine the best orders for delivering curriculum content? Should addition be taught before subtraction? Should students learn a particular skill before being introduced to a new, and often related skill? Another project currently taking place within ASSISTments is helping to strengthen PLACEments, an infrastructure that helps teachers to identify and target their students' learning gaps. Researchers are currently examining how to pinpoint the best order of instruction for Common Core Mathematics skills within ASSISTments. Your participation will help us improve a number of features in ASSISTments so that we can better serve our teachers and students. Please click here to participate in a brief survey to help drive our research.
