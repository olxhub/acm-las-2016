In order to test the model, it was necessary to determine whether the underlying conepts and definitions were appropriate and shared by others in the community.

In the first part of this 'flipped poster', we asked you to enter your own definitions of four different terms: 1) student experience; 2) student engagement; 3) student satisfaction and 4) student evaluation of teaching.

Below you can see the snapshot of current submissions.

From the variety of responses above, it is possible to notice three patterns:

  • there is some consistency in the definitions for the same term
  • there are similarities between the responses in different definitions (room for interpretation)
  • there is a great variety of interpretations, focus, and scope
