Our Data

The data in this file only includes students who signed up for the course in time to participate in our survey. Many more students joined the course after this deadline. To earn a certificate in this course a student needed a 60% average on the quiz and homeworks. In our analysis we counted students as participating if they got a score greater than zero.

Data Fields:

  • Anonymized User Id: A unique number for each user
  • Answered pre-course survey: True if the student answered the pre-course survey
  • Quiz Week 2: The student's score on a quiz with a maximum score of 10
  • Homework 3: The student's score on an assignment with a maximum score of 100
  • Homework 4: The student's score on an assignment with a maximum score of 100
  • Homework 5: The student's score on an assignment with a maximum score of 100
  • Homework 6: The student's score on an assignment with a maximum score of 100
  • Homework 7: The student's score on an assignment with a maximum score of 100
  • Homework 8: The student's score on an assignment with a maximum score of 100
  • Answered post-course survey: True if the student answered the post-course survey
  • In Signature Track: 1 if the student was in the signature track
  • experiment_1: True if the student was in the first experimental group
  • control_1: True if the student was in the first control group
  • experiment_2: True if the student was in the second experimental group
  • control_2: True if the student was in the second control group
  • Community TA Interest Result: 1 if the student said yes, 0 if they said no
  • TA Survey answer time: The time a student answered the community TA survey
