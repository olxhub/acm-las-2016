Welcome!

This paper is the result of my experience in the classroom as a middle school math teacher. I have used a variety of web-based programs to help my students learn math. I was curious about how systems determine the amount of practice provided to students and how accurately they predict mastery.

About the Authors:

Kim Kelly is a PhD student in the Learning Sciences and Technology program at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. She is also an Instructional Coach at Southbridge Middle High School in Massachusetts. Her major research interests are in self-regulated learning.

Neil Heffernan is the co-creator of ASSISTments and the director of the Learning Sciences and Technology program at Worcester Polytechnic Institute.
