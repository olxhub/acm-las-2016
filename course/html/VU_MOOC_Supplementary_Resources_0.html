The results were not significant in most cases, except that the completion rate of the experimental group on the very last assignment of the course was higher than the control group.

We had thought that learners might be more motivated to complete the assignments shortly after the intervention, and that this effect would fade away. But this did not seem to be the case.

We have introduced one of many possible lightweight interventions in open, online courses. Our future goals are to repeat and elaborate the intervention described here, and to explore more of these in both online courses and blended courses.

Having asked learners about their interest in becoming a community TA, we will indeed be considering the top 20% for this role in upcoming sessions.
