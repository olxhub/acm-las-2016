e-Tutor is comprised of 14 modules, with Moodle used as the Learning Management System, each covering a different aspect of teaching online considered the must-have competencies of online instructors. Each module uses a wide range of materials to ensure engagement of learners: handouts, narrated presentations, narrated videos, audios, interactive activities, e-portfolio artefacts and other Web resources.

e-Tutor provides support to students in the areas of:

(1) Fundamentals of e-Learning; (2) Online Learning Theories; (3) Learning and Content Management Systems; (4) Virtual Classrooms Management; (5) Online Instructional Design; (6) Online Instructional Methods; (7) Copyright, Digital Rights Management (DRM) and Plagiarism; (8) e-Assessment; (9) Graphical Design; (10) Creating Effective Visuals; (11) Multimedia Content Development; (12) Creating Interactive Applications; (13) Social Media and Emerging Technologies; and (14) Quality Control.
