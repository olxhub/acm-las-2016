Welcome to the 'MOOC discussion content analysis' course!

In this course, you will learn:

  • An overview of discussion topic modeling
  • Discussion topic labeling
  • An overview of topic visualisation

This course is intended for anyone interested in analysing massive discussion contents to identify the latent discussion topics.

It will take 5-10 minutes to complete the course and additional resources are provided for anyone wish to explore further.
