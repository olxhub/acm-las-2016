Cross-MOOC Classification Performance

Table 3 includes the cross-domain MOOC accuracy where a model in trained on the forum posts from a single course and then validated with unseen forum posts from the other two courses. The cross-domain performance varies across categories. The sentiment cross-domain MOOC classifiers achieve the best performance (the lowest accuracy being 0.62) while the urgency and confusion classifiers are unable to produce consistent generalisable performance for MOOC courses from a different domain. The lowest accuracy is achieved for confusion classification (i.e., 0.25).

Inspection of the top features (words) in each category, revealed the reason for low performing cross-domain classification results:

  • The top features for confusion and sentiment are highly related to the context in which the confusion or sentiment was expressed.
  • Confusion included words for the concepts that were found to be confusing
  • Urgency was associated with technology issues that students may have required an urgent response
