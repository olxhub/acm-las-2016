Hello everyone! Welcome to the wonderful world of Learning Transfer at scale!

The main goal of this mini-MOOC is to get you acquainted with our paper, Learning Transfer: Does It Take Place in MOOCs? An Investigation into the Uptake of Functional Programming in Practice. This is meant to prepare you for and get you excited about the flipped session we will be hosting at this year's ACM Learning at Scale conference.

Let us begin with our three Learning Objectives for you. After having completed this mini-MOOC, you should be able to:

  • describe what learning transfer is;
  • understand the research methods we adopted to measure learning transfer in our specific use case;
  • generate new ideas of how to investigate learning transfer in a wider range of MOOCs.

To get the most out of this mini-MOOC, we suggest you spend 20-25 minutes engaging with our materials offered here.
