Abstract

We are exploring the effects of social incentives and motivation on learner performance in a massive open online course.

In this flipped poster we describe a study where we asked learners if they wanted to be considered for a community TAship in a subsequent offering of the course, if they finished in the top 20% of those who completed the current course instance.

Our hope is to find small interventions like this that can influence learner performance without requiring the class to be redesigned.

After describing our study we ask you to give your intuition about what will happen and then report our results.
