Here, we present a sequence of videos to demonstrate  our peer assessment tool.

While it is not necessary to completely watch these videos it might be interesting as a reference for a better understanding of some of the details of our paper. 

We show on the teacher side how to 

  • create a peer assessment
  • create training samples
  • manage submissions
  • create a gallery

We also show the learner's view of 

  • training
  • submitting a solution
  • reviewing the work of peers
  • read the results
